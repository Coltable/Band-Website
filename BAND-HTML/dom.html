DOM MCLENNON.

Dominique Michael Simpson, better known as (Dom McLennon) is a American rapper, songwriter, and record producer best known for being a member and rapper of the American boyband BROCKHAMPTON.

Simpson was born and raised in East Hartford, Connecticut as he became interested in music when he began playing instruments such as saxophone, pianos, and drums when he was young. Simpson soon developed a love for literature and would soon end up going to school studying journalism while also developing his solo career. Simpson began his solo career back in 2012 as a year later, he released his debut album titled Thesis which is available on the music website Bandcamp.

Before making the move from Connecticut to Texas, Simpson would have online conversations and collaborations with Kevin Abstract, Ameer Vann, producer Romil (who is also from Connecticut) as he would later join the group after realizing the chemistry he had with the group.
