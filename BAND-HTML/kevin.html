KEVIN ABSTRACT.

Clifford Ian Simpson, better known as Kevin Abstract, is best known for being the leader, rapper, and creative consultant of American boyband BROCKHAMPTON. Aside from BROCKHAMPTON, he is also known for his solo albums MTV1987, American Boyfriend: A Suburban Love Story, and ARIZONA baby.

Born in Corpus Christi, Texas, Simpson was born to two Mormon parents and had an older sister, though he never knew his father and was never close with his mother. Simpson started recording and producing music at the age of 15. When he turned 17, Simpson left his home, moved to Georgia to live with his sister and started recording music with his longtime friends Ameer Vann, Dom McLennon, Mic Kurb, and his in-house producer, Romil.

In 2014, Simpson released his debut studio album MTV1987, which was met with critical acclaim from various music blog websites.

Aside from his solo work, Simpson is best known for being the founder, rapper, creative consultant, and official leader of the alternative American boy band BROCKHAMPTON as the collective has released five albums, a mixtape, and had a television show American Boyband which aired on Viceland.
