JOBA.

Russell Boring, better known as (JOBA) is a American singer, rapper, songwriter, sound engineer, and record producer best known for being a member, rapper, singer, audio engineer, and producer of the American boyband BROCKHAMPTON.

Boring was born in Houston, Texas which is where he spent a majority of his childhood. He met his future band members Kevin Abstract, Ameer Vann, Matt Champion, and Merlyn Wood at Woodlands High School though he was a year above them. After graduating from high school, he would continue to work with Kevin, Matt, and Ameer on their early music as their audio engineer.

In 2015, Boring would be asked by Kevin Abstract to join "BROCKHAMPTON" which he accepted, he would move in a house in San Marcos, Texas with the group while also attending school for vocal performing. After the group relocated to Los Angeles, Boring would continue to mix and master all of the collective's music while also being a main vocalist.
