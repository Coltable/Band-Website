MATT CHAMPION.

Matt Champion is a American rapper, singer, and songwriter best known for being a member, rapper, and singer of the American boyband BROCKHAMPTON. He is also known for appearing on Kevin Abstract's "SNAKES", along with his solo singles such as "Fangs", "Mansions", and "El Dorado".

Champion was born and raised in the Woodlands, Texas. During his time in high school, he made music solely before meeting his future band members Kevin Abstract, Ameer Vann, Merlyn Wood, and JOBA at Woodlands High School as they would continue to make music together all the way up to the formation of BROCKHAMPTON.

After joining BROCKHAMPTON in 2015, Champion released his first two solo singles: "Burn" and "You're Too Cool For Me" and proceeded to release a numerous amount of singles until the release his surprise album "HARLEY" in March 2017 which has been removed from all online streaming platforms. Not much is known about Matt's personal life as he prefers to keep low-key profile.
