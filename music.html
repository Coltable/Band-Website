GINGER

RELEASE DATE
August 23, 2019

GINGER is the fifth studio album of American boy band BROCKHAMPTON. It was released on August 23, 2019 by the band's label Question Everything, Inc. and RCA Records.
It is also the second album that was released in their "THE BEST YEARS OF OUR LIVES" trilogy, with the first being "iridescence".
The album was supported by the singles "I BEEN BORN AGAIN", "IF YOU PRAY RIGHT", "BOY BYE", "NO HALO", and "SUGAR.

SATURATION 3

RELEASE DATE
December 15, 2017

SATURATION III is the third studio album of American boy band BROCKHAMPTON and the third/final album in the SATURATION trilogy. The album was released on December 15, 2017 by Question Everything, Inc. and EMPIRE Distribution. The album features singles such as "BOOGIE", "STAINS", and "RENTAL".
Though it was originally announced to be their "final studio album", the group revealed that they will continue to release albums. The album peaked at #15 on the Billboard 200, making it the first album by the group to be featured in the Top 20.
